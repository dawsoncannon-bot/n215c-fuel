## Tank Position Reference

Available positions:
- **L TIP** - Left wing tip tank
- **L MAIN** - Left main wing tank
- **CENTER** - Center/fuselage tank
- **R MAIN** - Right main wing tank
- **R TIP** - Right wing tip tank
- **AFT** - Aft/auxiliary tank

## Important Notes

1. **USABLE FUEL ONLY** - Not total tank capacity
2. **Verify from POH** - Don't guess or estimate
3. **Specify variants** - Same aircraft can have different configs
4. **Include notes** - Context helps users understand differences
